These estimators are easy to implement and low variance, but the goal of scaling them comprehensively to large combinatorial distributions is still outstanding. We introduce stochastic softmax tricks, which generalize the Gumbel-Softmax trick to combinatorial spaces. Our framework is a unified perspective on existing relaxed estimators and contains novel relaxations. We design structured relaxations for subset selection, spanning trees and arborescences. We find that stochastic softmax tricks can be used to train latent variable models that perform better and discover more latent structure than less structured baselines." track: research live: false ---
